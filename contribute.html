Build Mods, Collect Bounties

Dragon is a trading companion divided into data-modules that visualize the deceptions and power concentrations on a Solana memecoin. Currently these modules produce analyses on bundles, snipes, clusters, and top 10 holders. Soon Dragon will produce analyses on many other deception techniques, crowd-sourced by the builders and traders in the trenches.


First Task: We build the data-pipelines to Dragon’s first four mods. The front-end for each mod is already built for proof-of-concept, and gathers data by web-scraping various online tools. The successful integration of a Solana node RPC pipeline into one of these mods will reward the developer with the bounty specified.
